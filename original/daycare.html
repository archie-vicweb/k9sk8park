Daycare

Assessments are done Monday to Friday from 11 am to 2 pm by appointment. Must provide Proof of Vaccination records. Males must be neutered if over the age of 6 months, and females can not come in while in season. For more details, please read below, or call our daycare department at
​#778-265-2002  
We ask that you please let your dog relieve him or herself before arriving at K9SK8 Park! There are some green spaces around the facility , please clean up after your four legged friend! If in need of a poop bag, we have LOTS! Ask a staff member .
Some of our K9 SK8 Park Family having fun!

Daycare is a wonderful way to keep your dog entertained while you're busy, or at work. Daycare is also a great way to socialize puppies and to build confidence in dogs of all ages. If you dog has special bathroom needs, please let one of our staff know prior to leaving your dog, and we will try and accommodate those needs the best we can. We can also feed your dog Lunch or special medications throughout the day for no charge, just let a staff member know ahead of time!

- - > All new dogs must come in for an assessment before becoming a part of our K9 family. This involves an assessment of your dog's level of dominance, as well as its ability to read and express canine body language in a safe and appropriate manner. We will also check their vaccination records at that time.
* Meet & Greets are done between 11am - 2pm, Monday to Friday by appointment only *



Please see below for exact daycare requirements
including vaccines:
Daycare Requirements All dogs must be up to date
with their vaccinations:
Bordetella, distemper, canine parvovirus, canine parainfluenza, canine adenovirus and rabies are required.

All male dogs over the age of 6 months must be neutered.
Unspayed females must stay home for the entire duration of their heat.

All dogs must be licensed in order to go for walks.

Other Rules and Regulations: Safety is our main concern. As such, we ask that you refrain from trying to open any gates yourself. This shop is equipped with a triple gate system. Our staff will open gates for you when it is safe to do so.

When walking dogs, we use our own leashes and collars for maximum safety. Your dog will learn to walk on a loose lead. Training collars will always be used, as this is the only type of collar that cannot break open. We are more then happy to use your own harness if you prefer, but will have our leash and collar on, as a back up.

Payment is due at time of service.
​Ask about our pre-paid punch cards.


 Other Information :
Nuisance barking is not permitted, as it can agitate and greatly alter the mood of the other dogs. We do use citronella collars and spray water bottles as training aids to avoid this problem. 

We are happy to feed your dog meals (if supplied) and/or administer medication if necessary, for no extra charge.

Feel free to speak with one of our staff if you have further questions about daycare.
 Our goal at K9SK8 Park is to provide physical activity and mental stimulation for your dog! Dogs are pack animals, and daycare is a safe and fun way to allow your dog to experience the
benefits of pack structure.
